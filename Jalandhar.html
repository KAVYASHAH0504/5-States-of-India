Jalandhar is a city located in the north Indian state of Punjab. Jalandhar was ruled by the Hindus and the Mughals in succession and is believed to be the oldest city in Punjab. Jalandhar city was referred to as Prasthala and as Jullundur at the time of British Raj and is known for its rich cultural heritage, historical monuments and traditional values. Jalandhar is also known as the town of sports. Most of the sports materials that are manufactured in Jalandhar are exported to various countries. The city also has the distinction of producing some of the best sportsmen in the country. Many highly successful companies have grown up in the city and surrounding areas. Its steel and iron re-rolling mills, rubber goods, electric goods, automobile parts, and sewing machine factories and handloom products are also very famous. It is one of the biggest centres of the vernacular press in India.
